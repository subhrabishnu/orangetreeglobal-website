Our Mission

Democratizing AI for
Indian business

India has 63 million MSMEs. They generate 45% of industrial output and 40% of exports. Yet the vast majority have been locked out of AI adoption — by cost, complexity, and lack of relevant tools.

OrangeTree Global exists to change that. Our consulting practice, our AI SME Suite, and our training programs are all designed with one goal: making AI adoption practical, affordable, and high-impact for Indian MSMEs.

We don't believe in AI for AI's sake. We believe in AI that moves the needle — on revenue, cost, efficiency, and competitive advantage — for the kinds of businesses that form the backbone of India's economy.

Our Journey

17 years of building capability

2009

OrangeTree Global Founded — Kolkata

Started as a boutique data analytics training institute in Kolkata, offering weekend programs in Excel, SQL, and Business Analytics. First cohort: 18 professionals from banking and manufacturing.

2012

First Corporate Training Contract

Secured our first corporate training engagement — upskilling 60 analysts at a leading Kolkata-based NBFC in data analytics and statistical modeling. Expanded into Python and R programming.

2015

Machine Learning Programs Launched

Launched India's early weekend-format ML practitioner programs, designed for working professionals. Reached 500 alumni milestone. Began curriculum partnerships with industry practitioners.

2017

Consulting Practice Established

Formalized our AI and analytics consulting practice, moving beyond training to building actual models and analytics systems for corporate clients across BFSI, retail, and manufacturing.

2019

National Reach — 1,000+ Alumni

Expanded training reach across India through online delivery. 1,000+ alumni across Kolkata, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Delhi. Launched deep learning and NLP tracks.

2021

AI Consulting Scales to 50+ Clients

Grew consulting client base to 50+ organizations. Delivered production ML systems for fraud detection, demand forecasting, and customer analytics across BFSI and retail sectors.

2023

GenAI Practice Launch

Built our Generative AI practice following the LLM revolution. Integrated Claude API and GPT capabilities into client workflows. Began designing AI tools specifically for Indian MSMEs.

2025

OrangeTree AI SME Suite — 10 Tools

Launched the OrangeTree AI SME Suite: 10 ready-to-deploy AI applications purpose-built for Indian MSMEs — covering operations, compliance, finance, marketing, customer experience, and sales.

2026

MSME AI Adoption Platform

Officially repositioning as India's dedicated MSME AI adoption company — integrating consulting, tooling, and training into a single partnership model for SMEs across India.

What We Stand For

Principles that guide our work

Outcomes over outputs

We don't measure success by models built or slides delivered. We measure it by revenue generated, costs reduced, and hours saved for our clients.

Build capability, not dependency

Every engagement includes training. We want our clients to own their AI — understanding it, maintaining it, and evolving it without needing us every step of the way.

Honest about what AI can't do

We don't oversell AI. If a problem doesn't need AI, we'll tell you. Good consulting starts with honesty about when simpler solutions work better.

India-first, not imported

Our tools, our training content, and our consulting frameworks are built for Indian business realities — GST, DPDP, vernacular data, Indian regulatory context.

Practitioners, always

Every person on our team is an active practitioner — someone who builds, trains, and deploys AI systems, not just teaches or advises on theory.

Accessible, not exclusive

We consciously price and scope our work for MSMEs — not as a scaled-down enterprise product, but as something genuinely designed for businesses of 10–500 people.
