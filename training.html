Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered

Do you offer data science courses online in India?

Yes. All OrangeTree programmes are delivered live online — every Friday evening (6–8pm) and Saturday (6–8pm). Sessions are also recorded so you can revise at your own pace.

Do I need any prior knowledge to join?

Tracks 03 and 04 (AI for Business Leaders and Claude for Business) require zero technical background — they are designed specifically for non-technical professionals. Tracks 01 and 02 start from basics, though some familiarity with spreadsheets is helpful for Track 01 and basic programming awareness helps for Track 02.

How does the certification work?

After the live sessions end, you submit a case study assignment (within 1 week) and then take an online exam. On passing, you receive an OrangeTree Global certificate. There is no time pressure — you complete at your own pace after the sessions.

Do you offer corporate training for organisations?

Yes. All four tracks can be customised and delivered as corporate programmes — at your offices across India or online. We design the curriculum around your industry, your tools, and your business context. Contact us for a corporate quote.

What is the Claude & AI Applications for Business programme about?

It is a 4-week programme that teaches non-technical managers how to use Claude AI for real business tasks — with a dedicated session for Marketing, Sales, HR, Finance, Operations, and Strategy. No coding required. You leave with a personalised AI workflow for your own role.
