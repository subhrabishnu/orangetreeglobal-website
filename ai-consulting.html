Service 03

AI Integration & Deployment

The hardest part of AI is not building the model — it's making AI work inside your real business processes. We connect AI to your existing ERP, CRM, and operational systems so it becomes part of how you work daily.

What integration looks like

01
System audit
Map your current tech stack — ERP, CRM, databases, APIs — and identify integration points and constraints.
02
Pipeline development
Build data pipelines that feed AI models with live, clean data from your operational systems in real time.
03
API & UI integration
Embed AI outputs (scores, predictions, alerts) directly in the dashboards and tools your team already uses.
04
Monitoring & handover
Set up model performance monitoring and train your internal team to maintain and retrain models independently.

Systems we integrate with

Tally ERP SAP Zoho CRM Salesforce HubSpot Power BI Tableau Google Sheets MySQL / PostgreSQL WhatsApp Business API REST APIs Custom Legacy Systems

We build AI into your existing workflows — not parallel workflows your team has to remember to check. If your team isn't using it, we haven't succeeded.
